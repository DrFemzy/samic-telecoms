Our Vision, Mission and Values

For over a decade years, SAMIC has always been known as a giant in the telecommunication industry. In the year 2015 we became a registered company with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria and in the year after that, 2016, we were licensed by the Nigerian Telecommunication Commission.
